Best Practices of Design

  • If your layout becomes complex; take a step back to paper and pencil to plan out your next steps
  • Use whitespace liberally to show breaks in content and to frame your text to avoid lines over 80 characters

How to Implement with Materialize

  • Use of divs to section off areas of content
  • The grid in Materialize requires multiple classes to implement
  • Use the responsive width classes when placing items within rows in the Materialize grid
